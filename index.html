Biography

I am Shkodran Arifi and I live in Pristina, Republic of Kosovo. Currently working with Mozaix LLC.

I have graduated with bachelor’s degree on Communication Design and have had a vast experience with electronic design, specifically smart phones and web.

I have more than 6 years of designing experience and have a rich portfolio that you can check online. I engage my self all the time in trying to solve different user experience, be it on the streets, school or our daily apps that we use.

I have been convinced that my advantage is that I do not consider my job as a job that I have to do from 8 to 5 and then have fun; I am having fun while I’m working, so I don’t have to quit it, I already am having fun.
